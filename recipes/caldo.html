Caldo familia Pradilla

This colombian caldo de heuvo recipe is simple and quick, leaving everyone asking for more.

How to Make Caldo

Ingredients

Serves 5-6 people

  • 8 cups of Water
  • 8 Russet Potatoes
  • 4 green onion stalks
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Salt to taste

step-by-step

  1. Set water to boil
  2. Prep russet potatoes - peel, cut lengthwise and cut discs about 1cm thick and add to water once boiling
  3. Dice green onion and mince garlic and mix together
  4. Add a small amount of hot water from pot to onion garlic mixture and add about a teaspoon of salt. Mix and lightly mash to incorporate everything together. Add mix to pot.
  5. Wait for potatoes to cook ~ al dente. Pull one out and stick your finger through it. Should snap in half but still not very soft.
  6. Scramble eggs
  7. Mince Cilantro
  8. Add half cup of milk
  9. Slowly add eggs with a fork into pot while simultaneously stirring to create egg bits
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Boil caldo and turn off heat
